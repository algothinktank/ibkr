Wheel Story

Do this on Monday

1. Check what the cash balance is
    a. 'check_cash_balance.py'

2. Get current stock price (so you can calculate how many shares to buy)
    a. 'yahoo_check_px.py'
    b. 'IBKR_px_check.py'

3. Calculate number of shares_to_buy = cash_balance / stock_px
    a. 'calc_shares_and_contracts.py'

5. Buy the shares on Monday at 10 am
    a. 'buy_shares_at_particular_time.py'

6. check position

7. calculate total number of shares in portfolio

8. Calculate number of option contracts to sell = total_shares / 100

9. Sell the options for coming options expiration
    a. 'sell_options_at_particular_time.py'

10. Run the loop again coming Monday
